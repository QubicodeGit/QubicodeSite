Source File

Item #: SCP-087

SCP-087 is catalogued as a Euclid-class unlit platform staircase. The access door is disguised as a janitorial closet and secured with reinforced steel, an electro-release lock, and internal foam padding. Following Exploration IV, personnel access is prohibited.

Each flight descends to a semicircular landing before reversing direction. The structure limits visibility to roughly one and a half flights, absorbs excess light, and has no confirmed endpoint.

Anomalous Contact

SCP-087-1

Recorded explorations report distressed childlike vocalizations from far below the entry point, yet descent does not appear to close the distance. Class-D subjects also encountered SCP-087-1, a face-like presence with no visible pupils, nostrils, or mouth.

Addendum: After Exploration IV, campus personnel reported intermittent knocking from inside the sealed doorway. Reports ceased after additional industrial padding was fitted.

Overview

The descent does not answer back.

SCP-087: Exploration IV is a short atmospheric horror game focused on tension, darkness, and descent. The player takes the role of a D-Class subject sent into an endless concrete stairwell to document what lies below. As the exploration continues, the environment becomes increasingly hostile, distorted, and unsettling.

Concrete

Hard surfaces, repeating landings, and no useful sense of distance.

Darkness

The flashlight shows only enough to continue, never enough to feel safe.

Descent

Every flight repeats the order: lower, quieter, less certain.
